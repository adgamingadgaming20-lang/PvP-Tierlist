About This Tier List

This website shows the ranking of Minecraft PvP players based on their skill level and performance🏆. Players are placed into different tiers to represent their strength in competitive PvP🌍.

Each tier is divided into:

🥇HT (High Tier) – the strongest players in that level🥇.

🥈LT (Low Tier) – players who are still strong but slightly below the HT players🥈.

🔔Tiers go from HT1 / LT1 (best players) down to HT5 / LT5🔔.
